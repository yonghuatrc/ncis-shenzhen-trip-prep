🚀 Core Capabilities

Patient Journey — 7-Stage AI Lifecycle

  • Appointment — WeChat mini-program booking
  • Pre-Consultation — AI questionnaire (symptoms, history, allergies); doctor reviews before consult
  • Consultation — Report one-click analysis; Report Interpretation Assistant (medical imaging, lab results)
  • Health Screening — Add-on recommendation engine (Thyroid ¥90, Urine Microalbumin, Psychological ¥39)
  • Medication — Personalised plan (e.g. Aspirin 2 tabs + Rosuvastatin 5 tabs, 3× daily)
  • Medication Plan — Patient-friendly instructions, reminder scheduling
  • Follow-up — AI-generated follow-up questions based on diagnosis and patient profile

    🚀 Philosophical Takeaway

    AI is a staff-first enabler, not just a patient-facing tool. Tencent's model: give clinicians AI that saves them time, and the patient experience improves as a byproduct. "Build for the people delivering care, not just the people receiving it."
