🎮 4 Proposed NCIS Collaboration Domains

Domain 01 — Patient Management Agent
AI chatbot: booking, symptom triage, Q&R, follow-up, survivorship/community events, ChemoCalc cost guidance. For new patients, ongoing patients, and clinical trial patients.
Domain 02 — Clinical Documentation Support
AI drafts consultation notes, discharge summaries, and follow-up letters from doctor input — customisable via WorkBuddy to match NCIS templates. Doctor always reviews and signs off.
Domain 03 — Unified Patient Health Records
Tencent Report Analysis Agent consolidates patient reports and results from multiple visits/providers into one digital record, with a full patient timeline and free-text search.
Domain 04 — Tencent Omics + WorkBuddy
For researchers — the Omics Platform and WorkBuddy, both running on Tencent Cloud.

💬 NCIS WhatsApp Care Assistant — 8-Agent Backend

Multi-agent orchestrator underpinning the NCIS Care Assistant demo shown during the visit.

● Orchestrator agent — intent routing + free-text understanding
● Booking agent — schedule, reschedule, queue
● Referral & triage agent — new patient pathway, symptom flags
● Health records agent — consolidated reports & timeline Q&A
● Care companion agent — survivorship & community events
● Therapy consultation agent — grounded Q&A on treatment & care plan
● Financial counselling agent — ChemoCalc cost estimate
● Human handoff — nurse alert on red flags

Includes accessibility option (larger text) for elderly patients.

📋 7 Recommended Follow-Ups for NCIS/NUHS

  1. AI-assisted staff copilot — approved information + recommended actions, human decision-making stays
  2. Patient navigation — guide patients to correct service, appointment, information, care team
  3. Oncology patient education — standardised approved information, complex questions escalated to clinicians
  4. Longitudinal patient support — reminders, symptom monitoring, survivorship support across the care journey
  5. Privacy-preserving analytics — controlled data environments, de-identification, governed data tagging
  6. Enterprise AI governance — common model standards, approval processes, monitoring, audit, and exit criteria
  7. AI-native process redesign — select one workflow and redesign end-to-end, not just add a chatbot

🚀 Key Takeaway

Don't chase the most pilots. Build a governed, reusable, patient-centred AI capability that improves outcomes, supports healthcare professionals, and can operate safely within the Epic and Synapxe environment.

Tencent's strength is platform infrastructure — compute, model hub, agent orchestration, enterprise tools. Ping An's strength is operating discipline — AI as strategic infrastructure, governed and patient-centred.
