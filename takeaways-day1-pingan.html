🚀 Key Philosophical Takeaway

AI as strategic infrastructure — shared capability, not a project. Ping An builds governed, reusable AI platforms that improve outcomes, support employees, and operate safely within regulatory and institutional guardrails.

AI scale must work within governance, cybersecurity, and regulatory frameworks — not bypass them. The shift is from "Machine-Assisted Human" to "Human-Assisted Machine": business experts turning tasks into annotations that make AI smarter.
